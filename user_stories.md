### Epic: AI-Powered Video Metadata Generation

This epic covers the main goal of automating the creation of video metadata to improve efficiency and content performance.

#### User Stories:

1.  **Automatic Content Analysis**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** the system to automatically analyze the content of a video I upload **so that** its key themes and topics are understood for generating relevant metadata.

2.  **Thumbnail Suggestions**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** to be presented with 3-5 automatically extracted, visually appealing thumbnail options from my video **so that** I can choose the one with the highest click-through potential without manual searching.

3.  **Synopsis Generation**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** the system to generate a concise and informative synopsis of the video **so that** I don't have to write the description from scratch.

4.  **Title Suggestions**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** to receive one or more suggestions for an engaging, SEO-optimized title **so that** I can quickly choose the best one for my audience.

5.  **Review and Edit Interface**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** to see the AI-generated title and synopsis pre-filled in the publishing form and be able to select from the suggested thumbnails **so that** I can easily review, edit, and approve all metadata before finalizing the publication.

6.  **Seamless Workflow Integration**
    *   **As a** Content Creator, **I want** the AI generation process to start automatically in the background as soon as I upload a video **so that** the suggestions are ready for me when I get to the metadata editing step, saving me time.
